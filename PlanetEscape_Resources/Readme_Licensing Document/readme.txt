INSTRUCTIONS:- 

		How To Play:

			- Move: LEFT, RIGHT

			- Jump: SPACEBAR   
              
			- Health: Monitor the constantly depleting oxygen meter at the top of the screen to manage your health effectively, certain plants will replenish your oxygen but also shoot thorns so be aware of them.   
           
			- Enemy: Navigate with caution, avoiding dangerous plants, flying enemies, and treacherous pitfalls, as they pose an immediate threat to your astronaut's survival                

			- Goal: Return to the ship before your oxygen depletes to keep your astronaut alive

			- Score: The score decreases as you take more time to complete the level, the maximum score is 2000 if you finish in less than approximately 3 minutes and 30 seconds, complete the level quickly for a higher score



CHEAT CODES:-

		- Press "Shift" : Pauses the automatic oxygen depletion, as well as makes you immune to thorns being fired at you.

		- Press "I" : Makes you immune and invincible to the enemies, i.e. you do not get killed by them.

		- Press "H" : To view the High Scores.
